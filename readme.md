## Second Project - Mediacl Insurance Cost prediction(Regrssion)

- Goal: Predict individual medical costs using patient demographic and health data.
- Key Models: Linear Regression, XGBoost, and Random Forest.
- Results: Optimized a Random Forest Regressor to reach an R^2 of 0.855.
- Insight: Identified Smoking Status and BMI as the primary drivers of insurance charges

## Third Project - Customer Segmentation online shopping Analysis(Clustering)

- Goal: Group customers into distinct segments based on purchasing behavior to enable targeted marketing strategies.
- Key Algorithms: K-Means Clustering
- Methodology: Used the Elbow Method and Silhouette Scores to determine the optimal number of clusters.
- Results: Identified high-value "VIP" clusters and "At-Risk" segments, allowing for personalized business actions
